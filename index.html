Tracing My Roots

Back to the Africa

I have always been interested in tracing my roots and unfortunately hit a dead end when using the records available. I eventually took a DNA test and was able to narrow down the countries my ancestors originated from. My ancestors were prodominently West African, more specifically Nigerian. I would would to visit and have my family and I learn about the traditions and culture that we were never able to experience ourselves.

The Pipes are Calling

On the other hand, I have heard many stories about other parts of my ancestry. One portion that I have not only been able to confirm, but also trace is the Irish piece of my family. I assumed it ony existed on my father's side based on family stories, so I was shocked to see some Irish relatives pop up on my mother's side. This led me to uncover I family register that led into the 1700s and back to a family church. I would love to go once and retrace some of the areas where my ancestors lived.
